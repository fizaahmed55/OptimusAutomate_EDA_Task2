Key recommendations

  • Protect high-performing regions: allocate stock and marketing budget toward the strongest sales regions, especially West.
  • Improve margin discipline: review product lines and categories where sales are high but profit conversion is weak.
  • Control discount leakage: compare profit across discount bands before approving aggressive promotions.
  • Focus on profitable products: top-selling products should be checked against profit, not only revenue.
  • Reduce loss-making orders: investigate the 372 loss-making orders through discount, shipping, and product-cost analysis.
